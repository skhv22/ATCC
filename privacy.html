Privacy Policy

Introduction

ATCC (Automated Template Content Creator) is a desktop application developed by ALKYNE. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use ATCC.

Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal data from users. The application uses the Pinterest API solely to search for and download public video clips based on your search terms. No user credentials, browsing history, or personal identifiers are collected.

How We Use Data

Downloaded videos are stored temporarily on your local machine in the cache/videos/ folder for processing. Once you finish your session, you may clear this cache manually. We do not transmit your data to any third party outside of standard API requests to Pinterest for public content.

Security

All data processing happens locally on your computer. We recommend securing your system against unauthorized access. ATCC does not transmit any of your files to external servers, except for API calls to Pinterest to fetch public media.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at alkyne.music@gmail.com.
