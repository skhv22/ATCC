ATCC

Automated Template Content Creator for promo videos

What is ATCC?

ATCC is a local desktop application that automates the process of creating short promotional videos from Pinterest clips. Simply enter your search terms, track, and overlay assets, and ATCC will:

  • Find and download Pinterest videos matching your vibe
  • Sync and trim clips to your audio beats
  • Insert media and text into an After Effects template
  • Render final videos without manual intervention

Perfect for social media campaigns on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and more.
